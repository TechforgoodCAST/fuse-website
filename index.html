Fuse

  • Type Funded programme
  • Client Comic Relief
  • Year 2016

To really accelerate the use of tech for social good, we need targeted programmes that support charities and nonprofit organisations to test and build digital innovations themselves.

In partnership with Comic Relief we're running the UK's first digital nonprofits accelerator. Over 2016 we'll work with 7 UK charities, wrapping a team of developers, designers, UX and business development around a charity team to enable them to create a sustainable digital product or service.

Click here to download more details about the programme

Digital Fellowship

  • Type Funded programme
  • Client Comic Relief
  • Year 2016

To transform the charity sector and enable it to put digital at the heart of its work, we need to support senior charity leaders and CEOs to understand the potential of digital.

Our Digital Fellowship, funded by Comic Relief, is a carefully constructed learning programme that teaches its fellows the key aspects of the digital development, from paper prototyping to business modelling. We bring the leaders together to learn from industry experts as well as spending time with key organisations leading the field. The programme culminates in a hack for the fellows to put what they've learnt into practice.

Check out our blog for more details about the programme

Age UK Islington and Islington Council

  • Type Advising
  • Client ufi charitable trust
  • Year 2015

We're working with Age UK Islington and Islington Council on the 'Realising the Value' project. A programme that is enabling service providers, commissioners and service users to collectively understand and assess the impact of wellbeing and prevention services happening across the borough.

Tech for Good Meetup Group

  • Partner
  • Bethnal Green Ventures
  • Find out more meetup.com/techforgood/

As a growing sector, it’s crucial that people find ways to come together, learn from one another and create opportunities - whether newcomers or those already dedicated to social tech. In partnership with Bethnal Green Ventures, we run the #techforgood meetups, bi-monthly events that inspire, connect and help #techforgood entrepreneurs get and give support.

Ufi Vocational Technology Programme

  • Type Advising
  • Client Ufi Charitable Trust
  • Year 2015

Post-16 vocational learning is of huge social and economic importance, yet it is often overlooked in favour of formal and academic education. The Ufi Charitable Trust is in a position to address this and we worked with them to find and support the most innovative uses of technology, and to create wide-spread positive change through vocational learning.
